About Me

I'm a cryptographer. My research focus is in the area of privacy and analysis of cryptographic protocols. My recent work includes studying and developing a state of the art Private User Discovery Protocol for anonymous communication systems. I have also been involved in implementing and validating cryptographic protocols in the blockchain space.
